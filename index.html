The LGBTQ+ in STEM Workshop is a two-day workshop on the experiences of LGBTQ+ people in STEM, with talks and workshops on themes such as community building, workplace organising, the experience of trans scientists, and how we can make change in the scientific workplace. It will be held on 22nd–23rd Sep 2026 at the Eddington Postdoc Centre at the University of Cambridge.

Registration for the meeting is required. Thanks to a grant from the Cambridge Research Culture Fund, only a nominal registration fee will be charged. Catering, including coffee breaks, lunch and dinner will be provided.
