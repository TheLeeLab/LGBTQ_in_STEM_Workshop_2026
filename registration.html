Registration

Registration for the LGBTQ+ in STEM Workshop 2026 (22nd–23rd September 2026, Postdoc Centre, Eddington, Cambridge) is now open.

Thanks to a grant from the Cambridge Research Culture Fund, only a nominal registration fee will be charged. Catering, including coffee breaks, lunch, and dinner, will be provided.

Abstract submission

We are looking to highlight stories of how people have achieved positive change in their workplace. We want to know what has worked, what hasn't worked, who your allies and co-conspirators have been, and how you have navigated resistance to change.

We welcome abstract submissions for:

  • Short talk
  • Poster
  • Either or both

The deadline for registration and abstract submission is 31st July 2026 (23:59 BST).

For any enquiries, please contact lgbtstemworkshop@ch.cam.ac.uk
